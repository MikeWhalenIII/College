Michael J. Whalen III

About Me

Mike Whalen is from Port St. Lucie, FL, and he currently resides in Jacksonville, FL. Mike works with Taylor Engineering, Inc. as a Desktop Support Technician; along with desktop support, he also assists with various systems and network administrative task, software development, and he is the lead administrator for the in-house Linux High-Performance Computing (HPC) cluster.

Before coming to Taylor Engineering, Mike served in the United States Marine Corps for five years. While in the Marine Corps, he attended various leadership courses and was tasked with safeguarding the Nation's strategic nuclear assets in the United States.
